TaskCode

5. Handling Missing Data

TaskCode

6. Modifying Data

TaskCode
Rename columnsdf.rename(columns={'old': 'new'})

7. Aggregation & Grouping

df.groupby('category')['sales'].mean()
df.groupby('category').agg({'sales': ['mean', 'max', 'min']})
df.pivot_table(values='Sales', index='Region', columns='Month')

8. Sorting

df.sort_values(by='col', ascending=False)
df.sort_index()

9. Date & Time

df['date'] = pd.to_datetime(df['date'])
df.set_index('date', inplace=True)
df['2023-01':'2023-12']

10. Merging & Joining

pd.merge(df1, df2, on='id', how='inner')   # Merge
pd.concat([df1, df2], axis=0)              # Stack vertically
pd.concat([df1, df2], axis=1)              # Combine horizontally

11. Saving Data

df.to_csv('output.csv', index=False)
df.to_excel('output.xlsx', index=False)

12. Tips

  • Use inplace=True to modify DataFrame in place
  • Use .copy() to avoid view/copy warnings
  • Use .query("col > 10") for cleaner filtering
  • Use .value_counts() to explore categorical data
